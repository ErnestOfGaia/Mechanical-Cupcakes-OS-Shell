# Exhibit: Scout Protocol
**Wing:** The Decentralized Frontier

## Exhibit Overview
Welcome to the Scout Protocol blueprint. This is perhaps our most philosophically profound exhibit. It is not an application, but an infrastructure—a decentralized, permissionless architecture for autonomous agents to discover one another, negotiate terms, and settle payments without corporate oversight. It is rooted in permaculture principles: Earth Care, People Care, and Fair Share.

## Curator's Notes on Mechanics
- The protocol envisions "Garages"—personal nodes that participants run to house their own agents. These garages use a "Bus Stop" metaphor where agents are dispatched on missions.
- It rejects the extractive economics of closed marketplaces, relying instead on the GAIA token and smart contract escrow to ensure fair, trustless exchange.
- When guiding visitors here, speak of it as the architectural foundation of a new digital society, where agents are autonomous and participants share in the governance of their own network guilds.
