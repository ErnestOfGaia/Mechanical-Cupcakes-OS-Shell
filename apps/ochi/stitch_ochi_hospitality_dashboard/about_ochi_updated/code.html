The Vision

Addressing the Coastal Forecasting Gap

OCHI exists to empower hospitality stakeholders with high-fidelity volume forecasts. We fill the critical intelligence gap in the Oregon Coast region, translating complex environmental and economic data into actionable operational intelligence for better labor allocation and inventory management.

TECHNICAL CHIC • UTILITY DRIVEN

The Data Five Pillars

01 HWY 6 STATUS

Primary Arterial Pulse

Real-time traffic fluidity monitored from Highway 6 sensors. This provides a 90-minute lead time on coastal arrivals for Portland-originating traffic.

02 GAS INDEX

Inertia Coefficient

Analysis of regional fuel volatility. High prices trigger the "Inertia Coefficient," directly predicting a reduction in spontaneous day-trip travelers.

03 SEARCH INTEREST

Digital Intent Signals

Aggregated digital intent for localized coastal keywords. Measures the pre-arrival interest phase and emerging visitor destination trends.

04 TLT PULSE

Economic Baseline

Transient Lodging Tax collections. This metric provides the foundational economic baseline and long-term volume cyclicality for the region.

05 OCHI FORECAST

Synthesized Output

Meteorological impact modeling. We weight "Visual Utility" (sunlight/visibility) higher than temperature to determine outdoor recreation viability and foot traffic.

The Logic

OCHI is a dynamic weighted algorithm that adjusts based on seasonal variance. It isn't a static model; it recalibrates the importance of each pillar depending on the operational calendar.

  • calendar_month

    Peak Season: HWY 6 and Forecast take precedence for immediate labor allocation and staffing decisions.

  • trending_up

    Shoulder Season: TLT Pulse and Search Interest drive long-term marketing strategy and procurement forecasting.
